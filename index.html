PROJECTS

User Interface

COSY.LAND

Whilst completing her degree in English and Media, Martyna decided to venture into GUI design after co-founding an online platform called COSY. To expand the platform and create a space for COSY’s growing audience from social media, Martyna and the COSY Team decided to develop a website.

Branding

COSY.LAND

In early 2020, in the midst of her studies, Martyna set out to create an online magazine which would turn into COSY, a tech-for-good social enterprise focused on empowering independent artists using innovative technologies. Martyna created a brand identity, including logo, primary colours, lettermarks, fonts and overall values which COSY was to exhibit in its design.

Social Media

COSY.LAND

In order to sustain COSY’s social media presence, Martyna has created over 250 infographics, promo posts and stories to communicate with her audience and showcase the writers and artists who contribute to the COSY community, curating an audience of around 12,000 followers across all social media platforms.

Animation

COSY.LAND

Martyna has predominantly used Adobe Animate and Procreate for the animations seen on the COSY website and social media platforms. With a focus on 2D frame-by-frame animations, she has been able to consistently produce walk cycles, motion graphics and promotional animations for the characters in the COSY universe.

Animation

Blueline Learning

Working with Blueline allowed Martyna to explore animation in a professional setting by creating unique motion graphics to be featured in videos for clients such as the West Yorkshire Police. As these animations are featured in educational training videos for law enforcement and fire departments, they must portray the message of the video in a clear and precise way.

ABOUT

Martyna Runkowska

(she/her/they)

Martyna Runkowska is a designer based in Sheffield, UK, specialising in branding, web design, animation, social media assets and web design and development.

In 2020 she co-founded COSY, a social enterprise focused on empowering independent artists using innovative technologies. COSY is a safe space for writers, visual artists, musicians and podcasters to share their work with a welcoming and open-minded community cultivated through social media and the COSY website.

Martyna is the Design Director of COSY, which showcases her versatility in designing a brand from the ground up, managing a successful social media page and designing web and social media assets in the form of illustrations and animations throughout COSY’s online presence.
